VK Global
Overview

VK Global, established in 2020, operates within the socio-commerce industry, leveraging a global framework to create impactful business opportunities. Partnered with leading corporate entities, we currently operate in the US and India, with plans for global expansion. Our mission is to help individuals monetize their social media presence by building entrepreneurial communities that generate revenue through growth and profit-sharing..

We are seeking ambitious individuals who are passionate about entrepreneurship, eager to learn new skills, and committed to building a business alongside their current careers. Our model offers the potential for creating passive income, providing financial security. Through leadership, communication, and dedicated mentorship and coaching, we empower people to grow their business, make an impact, and achieve success on their own terms.

Our Mission:
Empower individuals to build entrepreneurial communities and achieve financial independence through socio-commerce.
