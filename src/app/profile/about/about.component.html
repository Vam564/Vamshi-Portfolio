About Me

I am a passionate software developer with a strong background in web application development. My journey in tech began back home in India, where my interest in building digital solutions led me to pursue a bachelor’s degree in computer science. After completing my master’s in the U.S., I started working as a Software Developer, building and delivering web applications that solve real-world problems. Along the way, I’ve always believed that learning is a never-ending process, and I embrace the value of mistakes as stepping stones to success.

While I enjoy the challenges and growth opportunities in my full-time role, I’ve always harbored a dream to create something of my own. From a young age, I envisioned starting my own company, not confined to any particular industry. Recently, I took a step towards realizing that dream by founding VK Global with my wife Keerthana. We are building a leadership-driven business in the socio-commerce industry, aiming to create a lasting impact on people's lives.

Balancing my entrepreneurial pursuits with my full-time job, I’m committed to building a future where we break free from the traditional 9-5 path. Through continuous learning and leadership, I strive to build a business that drives personal and collective growth.
